Why is this required?

SPF (Sender Policy Framework)

Prevents unauthorized users from sending mail on behalf of your domain by listing authorized IP addresses.

DKIM (DomainKeys Identified Mail)

Adds a cryptographic signature to every email, verifying that the message was sent by your domain and wasn't altered.

DMARC

Tells receiving servers how to handle mail that fails SPF or DKIM, significantly boosting sender reputation.
