How it works

Our orchestration engine manages the full lifecycle of a support ticket, from initial ingestion to final human approval.

1

Webhook

Connect your support alias. We ingest tickets via secure webhooks and IMAP/SMTP protocols instantly.

2

Analyze

AI interprets intent and sentiment. Every message is parsed for urgency, technical depth, and customer mood.

3

Policy

Cross-reference with your support docs. The system pulls relevant context from your knowledge base to ensure accuracy.

4

Approve

Human-in-the-loop final review. Agents review the drafted response, making tweaks where needed before sending.
